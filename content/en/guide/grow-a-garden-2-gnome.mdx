*   **Server Lag:** If the "Einstein Event" was recently triggered, the server may have a cooldown before another gnome event can occur.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where is the best place to find a grow a garden 2 gnome?
The most reliable place to find a **grow a garden 2 gnome** for beginners is the Starting Garden area, where they have an 85% spawn rate. For advanced players seeking rare variants, the Cloud Island during the Golden Hour is the premier location, though the spawn rate is much lower at 5%.

### What does the Albert Einstein event do in Grow a Garden 2?
The "Einstein Event" is a rare, meme-inspired occurrence triggered by interacting with a high-rarity gnome. It causes chaotic visual effects and loud audio, but more importantly, it grants a temporary 3x multiplier to all harvest yields and coin generation for a short period.

### Can I trade my gnomes with other players?
Yes, gnomes are tradable items in Grow a Garden 2. Many players use the trade chat to swap duplicates or trade common gnomes for rare seeds. It is a vital part of the game's economy, especially for those looking to complete the "Zen Circle" synergy which requires eight gnomes.

### Do gnomes die or disappear over time?
Standard gnomes placed in your garden are permanent unless you choose to remove or sell them. However, "Wild Gnomes" found in the world have a despawn timer. If you don't interact with a wild **grow a garden 2 gnome** within a few minutes of it appearing, it will vanish and respawn elsewhere on the map.
