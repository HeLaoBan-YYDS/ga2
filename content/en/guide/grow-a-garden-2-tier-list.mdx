## Understanding Mutations and Their Impact

Beyond the base value of seeds, mutations play a critical role in boosting your profits in *Grow A Garden 2*. These special effects can significantly multiply the value of your harvest.

*   **Golden Mutation:** Increases fruit value. Some pets, like the Golden Dragonfly, can boost the chance of this mutation.
*   **Rainbow Mutation:** Significantly increases fruit value, often more than Golden. The Unicorn pet is specifically designed to enhance the likelihood of Rainbow mutations.
*   **Electric Mutation:** A rare and incredibly powerful mutation, offering a 25x boost to plant value. Player experience suggests this is linked to "lightning event" weather.
*   **Frozen Mutation:** Provides a 3x boost to plant value. This mutation is often associated with "snowfall event" weather and can be applied by pets like the Ice Serpent.
*   **Struck & Bloodlit:** These are rarer mutations, with community reports indicating they might be tied to specific "admin abuse" weather events. Their exact multipliers are not widely leaked, but they are known to be highly valuable.

Always aim for mutations to maximize the ROI of your crops. Certain pets and events can help you achieve these lucrative boosts.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the best pets in the Grow A Garden 2 tier list for defense?
For robust defense, the Ice Serpent and Black Dragon are top-tier in the *Grow A Garden 2* tier list. They actively freeze or burn intruders. The Bee is also a good, more affordable defensive option.

### Which seeds offer the highest profit in Grow A Garden 2?
The S-tier seeds like Moon Bloom, Dragon's Breath, and Venus Fly Trap offer the highest base resale prices and excellent ROI in the *Grow A Garden 2* tier list. Pomegranate also stands out for its high value.

### How important are mutations for maximizing profits in Grow A Garden 2?
Mutations are extremely important. They can significantly multiply the value of your fruits, with Electric mutations offering a massive 25x boost. Investing in pets like the Unicorn (for Rainbow) or Golden Dragonfly (for Gold) can drastically increase your mutated harvest.

### Should I bother with D-tier seeds in Grow A Garden 2?
D-tier seeds like Carrot and Strawberry are only useful when you're brand new to *Grow A Garden 2* and have very limited funds. You should aim to replace them with C-tier or higher seeds as quickly as possible to start generating meaningful profits.
