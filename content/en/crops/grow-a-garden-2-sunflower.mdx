### Alternative Seed Acquisition: Rainbow and Golden Events

While direct purchase is the most straightforward method, *Grow a Garden 2* offers exciting weather events that can yield rare seeds, including the possibility of a Sunflower. These events are a free-to-play alternative for those looking to expand their legendary crop collection.

During specific weather events, seeds can rain from the sky. There are two key events to watch for:

*   **Rainbow Event:** This event can drop Rainbow Seeds. When planted, these seeds have a chance to produce any plant in the game, from common carrots to the highly sought-after Dragon Breath, and potentially even a Sunflower.
*   **Golden Event:** Similar to the Rainbow Event, the Golden Event can drop Golden Seeds, which also have a chance to yield various plants, including legendary ones.

**Important Considerations for Event Seeds:**

*   **Public Servers vs. Private Servers:** Player experience suggests that the spawn rate for these rare seeds is significantly higher in public servers. While you *can* participate in private servers, the chances of seeds spawning are much lower, making it a more challenging and time-consuming endeavor. In public servers, be prepared to compete with other players for these valuable drops.
*   **Nighttime Spawns:** Community reports indicate these seed-raining events typically occur during nighttime in-game and last for approximately two minutes. Stay vigilant during these periods!

By strategically planting Rainbow and Golden plants near your Sunflowers, you can significantly increase your chances of harvesting these highly profitable rainbow variations.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: How do I get a Sunflower in Grow a Garden 2?

**A1:** You can obtain a **Grow a Garden 2 Sunflower** seed primarily from the Seed Shop for 5,000,000 Sheckles or Robux (599 for 1x, 1,797 for 3x, 5,990 for 10x). It has a 1.7% chance to stock. Additionally, you might acquire a Sunflower seed from Rainbow or Golden weather events that drop seeds from the sky, especially in public servers at night.

### Q2: What is the value of a Grow a Garden 2 Sunflower?

**A2:** The average value of a **Grow a Garden 2 Sunflower** produce is 1,750 Sheckles. Its price-floor value is 1,579 Sheckles. This makes it a very profitable Legendary crop for consistent earnings.

### Q3: Can the Grow a Garden 2 Sunflower be multi-harvested?

**A3:** Yes, the **Grow a Garden 2 Sunflower** is a multi-harvest crop, meaning it will continue to produce fruit after its initial growth cycle. This provides a continuous income stream once planted.

### Q4: Are there any special types of Sunflower in Grow a Garden 2?

**A4:** While the basic Sunflower is already a Legendary crop, you can aim to grow a "Rainbow" or "Golden" version of its produce by cultivating Rainbow or Golden plants nearby. These special plants significantly boost the chance of your crops, including the **Grow a Garden 2 Sunflower**, yielding highly valuable rainbow fruits with a 40x selling multiplier.
