## The Defensive Prowess of the Venus Fly Trap

Beyond its striking appearance, the Grow a Garden 2 Venus Fly Trap boasts a unique and aggressive ability: it actively attacks players who get too close. This makes it an invaluable deterrent against would-be thieves lurking in your garden.

### How the Venus Fly Trap Defends

When an unauthorized player approaches, the Venus Fly Trap:

*   **Targets and Bites:** It visually locks onto the intruder and snaps its trap.
*   **Deals Damage:** The bite inflicts approximately 75% health damage to the player.
*   **Steals Sheckles:** In addition to health loss, the victim also loses a small amount of Sheckles.

A fascinating, albeit sometimes frustrating, detail is that the Venus Fly Trap can occasionally bite its own owner if you interfere with its defensive process. This highlights its indiscriminate nature when protecting your garden. Community reports suggest that the number of hits required to eliminate a player from a Venus Fly Trap has increased from two to three, making it slightly less lethal but still a formidable foe.

## Understanding Venus Fly Trap Appearance and Mutations

The Grow a Garden 2 Venus Fly Trap is instantly recognizable due to its distinct features. It sports a curved dark green stem, two flat leaves at its base, and a prominent trap head with a vibrant red interior and square-shaped teeth. The crop it produces is visibly stored within the open trap, adding to its unique aesthetic.

### Known Venus Fly Trap Mutations

Like many crops in Grow a Garden 2, the Venus Fly Trap can undergo mutations, altering its appearance. While specific visual details for all mutations are still being documented by the community, two known mutations include:

*   **Bloodlit**
*   **Electric**

Players are encouraged to contribute images of these missing mutations to the Grow a Garden 2 Wiki to help complete the gallery. Remember, according to confirmed developer information, a plant can only have one mutation at a time. If your Grow a Garden 2 Venus Fly Trap already has a mutation, it won't acquire another until the current one is harvested or removed.

## The Stealing Glitch and the Venus Fly Trap

In Grow a Garden 2, a peculiar glitch exists that allows players to steal fruits even after being defeated by a Venus Fly Trap. This exploit, while potentially game-breaking, highlights an interesting interaction with this protective plant.

### How the "Die and Steal" Glitch Works

1.  **Approach a Venus Fly Trap:** Intentionally get within range of an opponent's Grow a Garden 2 Venus Fly Trap.
2.  **Get Attacked and Die:** Allow the Venus Fly Trap to defeat your character.
3.  **Respawn and Steal:** Upon respawning, if timed correctly, you can still be holding the fruit you attempted to steal before being defeated.

This glitch means that even the formidable Grow a Garden 2 Venus Fly Trap isn't a completely impenetrable defense against persistent thieves. Players have noted this glitch needs to be patched, as it allows for the theft of expensive fruits from other players' gardens.

## Frequently Asked Questions About the Grow a Garden 2 Venus Fly Trap

### Q1: How much does a Grow a Garden 2 Venus Fly Trap cost?
A1: The Grow a Garden 2 Venus Fly Trap can be purchased from the Seed Shop for 7,000,000 Sheckles or various Robux bundles (799, 2,397, or 7,990 Robux). Its availability is limited, with only a 1.4% chance of being in stock.

### Q2: What does the Grow a Garden 2 Venus Fly Trap do?
A2: The Venus Fly Trap is a Mythic defensive crop. When a player gets too close, it bites them, causing approximately 75% health loss and a small amount of Sheckles to be dropped. It acts as a deterrent against thieves.

### Q3: Can my own Grow a Garden 2 Venus Fly Trap bite me?
A3: Yes, community reports indicate that the Venus Fly Trap can sometimes accidentally bite its owner if you interfere with its defensive process or get too close while it's active.

### Q4: Are there any glitches related to the Grow a Garden 2 Venus Fly Trap?
A4: Yes, there's a known glitch where players can still steal fruits even after being defeated by a Venus Fly Trap. This "die and steal" glitch allows players to retain the fruit in their inventory upon respawn.
