## What Makes the Grow a Garden 2 Gold Seed So Special?

The **Grow a Garden 2 Gold Seed** stands out as a "Legendary" special seed, a tier above most other planting materials. Its primary allure lies in its guaranteed "Gold mutation," which automatically applies to any crop it grows into. This mutation isn't just cosmetic; it multiplies the sell value of the resulting harvest by a staggering 15 times! Imagine turning a regular potato into a super-spud worth 15 times its base price – that's the power of this golden wonder.

The Gold Seed is a Legendary special seed in Grow A Garden 2. When planted, it grows into a random crop but guarantees the Gold mutation, multiplying its sell value by 15x.

## Appearance

The Gold Seed appears as a golden, glowing seed with a metallic sheen. It radiates a warm, golden light that distinguishes it from regular seeds.

## Obtainment

Gold Seeds can be obtained through:

*   **Midas Moon Event:** Rare weather event where a Gold Seed can spawn
*   **Seed Packs:** Low chance from premium packs
*   **Events:** Special in-game events and promotions

## How It Works

*   Plant the Gold Seed in any available plot
*   The seed will randomly select any crop type to grow
*   When fully grown, the crop is automatically Gold variant (15x value)
*   Can be combined with weather mutations (Frozen/Electric) for even higher value

## Strategy Tips

*   **Wait for weather:** Plant during Snowfall or Lightning for multi-mutation stacking
*   **Friend boost:** Have friends nearby when harvesting for extra value
*   **High base crops:** The seed's random outcome means you could get any crop - maximize potential value
