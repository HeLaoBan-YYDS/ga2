## Protecting Your Precious Acorns from Thieves

One of the most significant new features in *Grow a Garden 2* is the introduction of a stealing mechanic. Unlike its predecessor, your hard work can be undone by other players if you're not careful. This adds an exciting, albeit sometimes frustrating, layer of strategy to the game. Protecting your **Grow a Garden 2 Acorn** crops is paramount.

### Understanding the Stealing Mechanic

Community reports indicate that stealing primarily occurs when a player leaves their garden. While you are actively present in your garden, your crops are safe. The moment you depart, however, your plots become vulnerable to opportunistic thieves. This dynamic forces players to make strategic decisions about when and how long to leave their garden unattended.
